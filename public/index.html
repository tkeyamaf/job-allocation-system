How CareerBridge Works

A fair, transparent system built to help people succeed

01

Fit Score Requirement

Each job has a minimum fit score threshold. Candidates below the threshold are not allocated, ensuring quality matches for both sides.

02

Weekly Application Cap

Each candidate can apply to a maximum of 5 jobs per week. This prevents spam and keeps the process fair for everyone.

03

Job Capacity Limits

Each job posting accepts a maximum of 12 candidates per week to ensure manageable review queues for employers.

04

Company Weekly Limits

Companies can receive a maximum of 30 new candidates per week across all their open roles.

05

Cooldown Period

After applying to a job, a cooldown period applies before you can reapply to the same role. This ensures thoughtful applications.
